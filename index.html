Important information

This calculator:

  • Estimates how much super a single person should have today to be on track for a comfortable retirement
  • Is based on ASFAs benchmark for retirement spending, which you can read about here
  • Is an estimate only and should not be relied upon for making financial or product decisions

This calculator is for illustration only. It uses general assumptions and does not take into account your individual financial situation, goals, or risk profile. Do not rely on this estimate as financial advice.

The estimate is based on fixed assumptions about wage growth, investment returns, fees, insurance costs, and contributions. Actual results may vary due to changes in the market, your employment, legislation, fees, or personal circumstances.

For a precise assessment, review the assumptions carefully and consider seeking qualified financial advice.
