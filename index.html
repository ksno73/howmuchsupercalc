Disclaimer: This calculator provides general information only and does not take into account your personal objectives, financial situation or needs. It is not intended to be financial advice and should not be relied on to make financial decisions.

The results are estimates only and are based on a range of assumptions used in the model. These include assumed investment returns, contribution rates, fees, insurance premiums and inflation. These assumptions are simplified and applied consistently over time and do not reflect real-world variability.

The calculator assumes:
• a constant annual investment return, applied uniformly each year
• regular superannuation contributions with no gaps in employment unless input by the user
• fees and insurance premiums are applied at assumed or average levels and may not reflect your actual super fund
• contributions, fees and insurance remain broadly consistent over time unless changed
• current superannuation rules, tax settings and contribution caps remain unchanged

This calculator models outcomes using annual projections and does not fully reflect the impact of short-term market volatility, sequencing risk, or year-to-year fluctuations in returns.

Where a retirement target is shown, it is based on the Association of Superannuation Funds of Australia "comfortable" standard for a single person. This standard assumes:
• retirement at approximately age 65–67
• the individual owns their home outright
• spending patterns consistent with ASFA's definition of a "comfortable" lifestyle

This standard may not be appropriate for your circumstances. Different household types (e.g. couples), renting in retirement, or retiring earlier or later may require materially different levels of superannuation.

This calculator has important limitations. It does not fully account for:
• variability in investment returns, including periods of negative returns
• changes in salary, employment patterns or contribution behaviour over time
• fund-specific fees, insurance arrangements or tax outcomes
• changes to superannuation legislation, tax rules or Age Pension settings
• individual strategies such as additional contributions, withdrawals or pension phase decisions

Actual outcomes may differ significantly from the results shown.

You should consider whether the assumptions used are appropriate for your circumstances and consider seeking professional financial advice before making any decisions based on this calculator.
